The Twelve Fundamentals.

Combine valuation, profitability, and balance-sheet filters to isolate the handful of names that actually deserve attention.

01P/E
Price to Earnings
Price-to-earnings ratio showing how much investors pay for each dollar of a company’s earnings.
Preset filters · Low <15 · Profitable >0 . High >50
02EPS
Earnings per share
Earnings per share, the portion of a company’s profit allocated to each outstanding share.
Preset filters · Positive · Negative · 0-10 · >25
03EV/S
EV to Sales
Enterprise value divided by revenue, measuring total company value relative to sales.
Preset filters · >0 · <0 . Low <1 · High >10
04ROE
Return on Equity
Return on equity, showing how efficiently a company generates profit from shareholders’ equity.
Preset filters · >0 · <0 · >30 · <−15
05D/E
Debt to Equity
Total debt divided by shareholders’ equity, indicating financial leverage.
Preset filters · <10 · <100 · >50
06Beta
Beta
Measure of a stock’s volatility relative to the overall market.
Filters · >0 · <0 . <1 · >1
07DY
Dividend yield
Dividends per share divided by price showing income return from dividends.
Filters · 0 . >0 · >5 . >10
08RG
Revenue growth
Total percentage increase in a given company’s sales over a certain period of time.
Preset filters · Positive · 0–10 · >25
09PM
Profit margin
Net income divided by revenue, showing how much profit is kept from each dollar of sales.
Preset filters · >0 · >20 · <−20
10GM
Gross margin
Revenue minus cost of goods sold divided by revenue, showing profitability before operating expenses.
Preset filters · >0 · >20 · <−20
11EV/EBITDA
EV/ EBITDA
Enterprise value divided by EBITDA, valuing the company relative to operating cash earnings.
Preset filters · >0 · >20 · <−20
12CR
Current Ratio
Current assets divided by current liabilities, measuring short-term liquidity.
Preset filters · >0 · >20 · <−20
